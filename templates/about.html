Welcome to Jakspot

Want to explore a city but don't want to hit up all the tourist locations?

Got a friend in town for the weekend and want to show them around but not sure where to start?

Jakspot has the answers to these questions.

Jakspot is the perfect space to share recommendations and find new places to explore. Whether it be a great place to watch the sunset or a hidden path in your local park, share your favorite spots with others and check out new ones. Go out, explore, and let us know when you've hit a Jakspot!
